About Us

Committed to Quality Education

Welcome to Government High School, Janai

Government High School, Janai, Raebareli is a prestigious government-run secondary school of Uttar Pradesh, established in the year 2012. The school is recognized by the Board of High School and Intermediate Education, Uttar Pradesh (Prayagraj) and provides education for Classes 9 and 10.

The school offers admission to both boys and girls. At present, 135 students are enrolled in the institution.

The academic activities of the school are conducted under the guidance of one Headmaster and four Assistant Teachers, who are dedicated to the all-round development of the students.

Teaching is carried out in accordance with the prescribed Uttar Pradesh Board curriculum, and students are provided with effective and systematic guidance for the High School Board Examination.

The primary objective of the school is to provide accessible, quality, and value-based education to students from rural areas.

Vision – Our Objective

To provide education that enables students to become knowledgeable, self-reliant, disciplined, and responsible citizens of society and the nation.

Mission – Our Goals

  • To provide quality secondary education
  • To develop discipline and moral values among students
  • To ensure strong academic preparation for board examinations
  • To provide equal opportunities to all students
  • To build a bright future through education
